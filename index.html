WikiPaw

WikiPaw (WKP) is a meme token bringing the power of decentralized, community-led currency to millions across the world. WikiPaw is a unique deflationary meme token with a total supply of 1 Billion, 100% ownership renounced and Liquidity pool locked forever.


WikiPaw is burnt at regular intervals to reduce total supply, this combined with a locked liquidity pool ensures a stable price increase.


FEATURES

Fair Launch


Available to everyone, Our wikipaw (WKP) is available to everyone on PancakeSwap V2.

Frequent Burn


10%, 15% and 20% of WKP will be burnt at different intervals within Phase 1 to Phase 4 as stated in the roadmap.

Community Driven


WKP economics are strictly community-driven. Contract ownership has been renounced and this places the power into the hands of the WKP community.

AirDrops


Frequent airdrops in controlled quantities to make WKP available to anyone and everyone. Keep an eye out!

FAQs

What is the Utility/Use Case for Wiki Paw?


WikiPaw is a meme token created by a team of crypto enthusiasts for a dog lovers' kids community to teach them how to create meme tokens. Regardless, we believe the use case for WikiPaw lies in the strength of its community that believes in the social consensus of shared value. This is what has taken the likes of Doge and Shiba Inu to billions of dollars in the market value.

Who owns WikiPaw?


WikiPaw ownership has been renounced. Ownership is collectively owned by the community. There is no CEO, no VC or board members pulling the strings in the background. For this reason, no wallet has more than 15% of the token. Funding or contributions are voluntary and ultimately the community gets to decide the future of the project.
